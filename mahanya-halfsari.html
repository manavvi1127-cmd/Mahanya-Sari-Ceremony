What is a Half Sari Ceremony?

The Half Sari Ceremony — known as Langa Voni or Pavadai Davani — is a beautiful South Indian coming-of-age celebration marking a young girl's transition into womanhood.

For the first time, the girl drapes a half sari: a skirt, blouse, and dupatta worn together — symbolising grace, culture, and the beginning of a new chapter. Family and friends gather to shower her with blessings, love, and joy.

Join us as we celebrate Mahanya on this radiant milestone! 🌸
